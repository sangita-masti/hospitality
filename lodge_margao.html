what lodge facility provide?

Hospitality Facilitation system provide Lodge facility it means accommodation for a period or a place to sleep for one or more nights.   Fancy hotels, youth hostels, and other businesses that provide a place for people to sleep overnight are all in the (lodge) Hospitality facilitation system.

  Recreation is any activity that people do for rest, relaxation, and enjoyment. The goal of recreation is to refresh a person's body and mind. Any business that provides activities for rest, relaxation and enjoyment, to refresh a person's body and mind is
  in the lodge.
